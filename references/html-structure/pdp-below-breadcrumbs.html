This Huffy Boys' 24 in Granite 15-Speed Mountain Bike takes you on your path to fun. The sturdy steel frame mountain bike features 15-speed index shifting so you can ride through the neighborhood, on gravel, in dirt and over hilly terrain! Sleek styling with the latest colors on a durable frame, this mountain bike delivers one great adventure!
